# NightCam

Advanced camera controls with long exposure, uncapped ISO and zoom for jailbroken iPhones.

## Features

- Long exposure from 1 to 30 seconds
- Uncapped ISO boost for extreme low-light
- Uncapped optical zoom beyond system limits
- Seamless integration into the stock Camera app
- Supports iOS 15 and above

## Compatibility

- iOS 15+
- iphoneos-arm64 only
- Requires MobileSubstrate, Substitute, or libhooker
